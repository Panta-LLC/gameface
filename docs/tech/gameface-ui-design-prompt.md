# Figma Design Prompt: GameFace UI

## Objective

Design an engaging and user-friendly interface for GameFace, a peer video gaming application that connects players, enhances gaming experiences, and fosters a vibrant gaming community.

## Target Audience

- **Primary Users**: Casual and competitive gamers looking to connect with peers.
- **Secondary Users**: Streamers, gaming influencers, and community organizers.

## Key Features to Include

1. **Home Screen**:
   - Personalized game recommendations.
   - Quick access to ongoing matches and friend activities.
   - Notifications for invites and updates.
2. **Matchmaking and Lobby**:
   - Seamless matchmaking based on skill level and preferences.
   - Lobby system for pre-game discussions and strategy planning.
   - Options for private and public game rooms.
3. **In-Game Overlay**:
   - Real-time video chat with peers.
   - Game stats and performance tracking.
   - Quick access to mute, volume, and other controls.
4. **Community Features**:
   - Forums and discussion boards for game-specific topics.
   - Leaderboards and achievement tracking.
   - Event creation and management for tournaments.
5. **Settings and Customization**:
   - Profile customization with avatars and themes.
   - Privacy settings for video and chat.
   - Game-specific preferences and controls.

## Design Guidelines

- **Style**: Immersive, dynamic, and gamer-centric.
- **Color Palette**: Vibrant and energetic colors with dark mode as the default.
- **Typography**: Bold and modern fonts, emphasizing readability and style.
- **Icons and Graphics**: Gaming-inspired and visually appealing.

## Deliverables

- High-fidelity mockups for the home screen, matchmaking, and in-game overlay.
- Interactive prototype showcasing user flows.
- Style guide including color palette, typography, and component library.

## References

- Platforms like Discord, Steam, and Twitch for inspiration.
- Modern gaming UI trends for immersive experiences.

## Notes

Feel free to innovate and propose creative solutions that enhance the gaming experience and community engagement.
